👩‍💻 About Me

I'm a Design Verification Engineer returning to semiconductors — with an additional layer of experience: two years of systematically built, deployed AI/ML expertise.

My DV foundation was built at Synopsys and Renesas Mobile, working on eMMC host controller and modem IP verification — constrained random environments, UVM testbenches, SVA/PSL assertion-based verification, CDC analysis with Spyglass, and low-power UPF flows.

Since 2023, I've retrained systematically across the full AI/ML stack — not just theory, but deployed systems. Starting with data analytics and cloud fundamentals, I progressed through MLOps, deep learning, NLP, and generative AI — each certification backed by an end-to-end portfolio project. Six projects. Six domains. All production-deployed.

Now I'm pointing all of it back at verification. I've completed a portfolio project applying ML to UVM functional coverage closure — treating simulation logs as a data pipeline, training a Decision Tree to predict uncovered bins, and auto-generating SystemVerilog soft constraints to close the loop into the next regression cycle and AI/ML-driven regression optimisation using open source tools.

The ability to work across both domains — writing the UVM environment and building the ML layer on top of it — is where I've chosen to focus, and what these projects demonstrate.

📌 Targeting mid-level DV Engineer roles at semiconductor companies, EDA vendors, and AI chip startups where verification and machine intelligence intersect.
