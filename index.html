Role Based Access Control Chatbot

RAG System

Business Problem:

  • FinSolve Technologies faced operational inefficiencies caused by communication delays and fragmented, siloed data across departments including Finance, Marketing, HR, and Executive leadership.
  • Departmental isolation limited timely access to relevant information, slowing decision-making, strategic planning, and project execution.
  • The organization required a secure, role-based AI solution to deliver on-demand, department-specific insights while enforcing strict access controls to maintain data confidentiality and operational efficiency.

Solution:

  • Designed and implemented a secure, multi-agent AI chatbot platform that extends beyond standard RAG by enabling live, role-aware knowledge access across continuously evolving data sources.
  • Enabled real-time document uploads (Markdown, CSV), dynamic creation of users and roles, and immediate availability of newly ingested knowledge for future queries under strict RBAC policies.
  • Developed a full-stack architecture combining a Streamlit UI for authentication, role management, and document ingestion with a FastAPI backend for business logic and AI orchestration.
  • Integrated an LLM-based query classifier to intelligently route queries to a SQL agent (DuckDB) for structured data or a RAG agent for unstructured documents.
  • Enhanced response reliability using embedding-based retrieval, reranking strategies, and fallback mechanisms.

Impact:

  • Scalability & Extensibility: Architected a modular, role-driven RAG system that supports dynamic onboarding of new users, roles, and documents, enabling seamless scaling across departments, larger datasets, and future AI agents without redeployment.
  • Innovation: Introduced an LLM-based query classifier that intelligently routes queries to SQL or RAG agents, combined with live document ingestion, metadata-driven RBAC, and fallback mechanisms to ensure reliable and efficient responses.
  • NLP Query Understanding: Delivered robust natural language understanding with context-aware retrieval, role-filtered responses, and effective handling of ambiguous or underspecified queries through semantic search and reranking.
  • Overall Business Value: Eliminated data silos and enabled secure, real-time access to evolving organizational knowledge, accelerating decision-making while maintaining strict data governance.

Adverse Drug Event(ADE) Detection & Severity Analysis

NLP System

Business Problem:

  • Mass COVID-19 vaccination programs generated large-scale Adverse Drug Event (ADE) data that is difficult to analyze due to noisy, unstructured clinical narratives.
  • Existing pharmacovigilance systems lack standardized severity labeling, contextual clinical details, and age-specific safety pattern discovery.
  • These gaps limit regulators and healthcare stakeholders from accurately assessing drug safety risks and making timely, evidence-based decisions.

Solution:

  • Developed ADEGuard, an AI-driven clinical text analytics system for automated ADE detection and severity assessment from unstructured reports.
  • Applied BioBERT-based NER to extract drugs and adverse events, combined with a hybrid severity classifier using rules and BioBERT fine-tuning with weak supervision.
  • Leveraged Sentence-BERT embeddings with t-SNE and K-Means to cluster and visualize ADE patterns across age groups and severity levels.
  • Integrated SHAP explainability to provide transparent, clinically interpretable severity predictions.
  • Delivered an interactive Streamlit dashboard with entity highlighting, severity views, clustering visualizations, and exportable insights.
  • Scalability & Extensibility: Designed a modular, dataset-agnostic architecture capable of scaling to large COVID-19 ADE datasets and integrating seamlessly into enterprise pharmacovigilance workflows and downstream analytics pipelines.
  • Innovation: Employed a hybrid clinical NLP pipeline combining BioBERT-based NER, weak supervision via Snorkel, and rule-driven post-processing to improve inference reliability while minimizing annotation cost and deployment complexity.
  • Extraction Accuracy: Achieved high precision, recall, and F1-scores for ADE and drug entity extraction on a gold-standard annotated COVID-19 test set, effectively handling ambiguous phrasing, overlapping entities, and complex clinical sentence structures.
  • Overall Business Value: Enabled instant ingestion of raw ADE CSV files, automatically generating structured safety reports and actionable insights segmented by drug, adverse event, age group, and predicted severity, supporting real-time decision-making and regulatory review.

Automated Car Damage Classification

Deep Learning

Business Problem:

  • Insurance companies, auto repair shops, and vehicle inspection services rely heavily on manual visual assessments of car damage, which are time-consuming and prone to subjective judgment.
  • Inconsistent evaluations can slow claims processing, increase operational costs, and introduce variability in damage appraisal.
  • There was a need for an automated, high-precision system to classify vehicle damage efficiently, improving workflow speed and consistency across stakeholders.

Solution:

  • Developed a supervised learning pipeline leveraging multiple Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) architectures to classify car damage into six distinct categories.
  • Implemented transfer learning using pre-trained ImageNet backbones to accelerate training and capture complex visual features.
  • Applied advanced data augmentation strategies, including geometric and color-space transformations, to improve model robustness and generalization.
  • Performed systematic hyperparameter tuning of learning rates, dropout ratios, and batch sizes to optimize convergence and validation accuracy.
  • Deployed a Streamlit-based interactive interface enabling real-time predictions, model testing, and rapid stakeholder feedback.
  • Integrated comprehensive evaluation tools, including confusion matrices and classification reports, for detailed insights into class-specific performance metrics.

Impact:

  • Operational Efficiency: Reduced manual inspection time, accelerating claims processing and damage assessments for insurance and repair workflows.
  • Consistency & Accuracy: Provided objective, reproducible damage classification, minimizing human subjectivity and errors.
  • Scalability & Deployment: Delivered a robust CNN-based solution with an interactive web interface for real-time deployment and stakeholder adoption.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Enabled detailed performance analysis per damage category, supporting continuous model improvement and informed business decisions.
