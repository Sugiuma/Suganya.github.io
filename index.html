About Me

I’m a seasoned professional with a strong foundation in ASIC design verification, now transitioning into the fields of Data Science, Machine Learning, and AI-driven software solutions. I bring a unique combination of hardware-level rigor, software development expertise, and automation testing experience, enabling me to approach problems with both depth and adaptability.

My career began with a deep interest in technology, which led me to work as a QA Engineer, where I developed a solid foundation in quality assurance, testing processes, and ensuring the reliability of software and hardware systems. I then earned a master's degree in VLSI Design and worked as a Verification Engineer. I specialized in verifying custom hardware systems such as Wireless Modems (2G/3G/LTE) and embedded MultiMedia Chips (eMMC), refining my skills in software development, quality control, and problem-solving.

In my earlier roles, I worked extensively on constrained-random verification, CDC analysis, formal methods, and logical equivalence checks, which shaped my precision-driven, analytical mindset. Today, I’m channeling that mindset into data science, intelligent automation, and AI-powered application development.

I’ve expanded my expertise with specializations in AWS fundamentals, Generative AI, and large language models (LLMs), all of which have equipped me with the tools to thrive in software development, testing, data analysis, or ML/AI engineering roles. With a proven track record of adaptability and a passion for continuous learning, I’m excited to bring my diverse skill set to new challenges in the tech space.
